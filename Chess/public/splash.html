How to Play

How to begin a game

You have to fill your name in the text box above the button. Then press Play the Game to connect to the server and be redirected to the game screen. You will have to wait for another player to connect in order to start playing.

Rules of connect4 are as follows:
Every turn you choose an empty place to fill with your color. You are allowed to fill a place only if the place below it is filled or if there is no place below. Click on the place you want to fill.

When do I win

When you have 4 filled places in a row, column or diagonal, you win.
